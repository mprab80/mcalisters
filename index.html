About Mcalister's Deli

McAlister's Deli is an American chain of fast casual restaurants founded in 1989 in Oxford, Mississippi, by retired dentist Dr. Don Newcomb. There are currently over 350 locations in 29 states. We offer delicious sandwiches, mouthwatering soups, craveable salads, and our signature ice cold Mcalister's Sweet Tea. It's a great place to have some delicious food and relax, so check it out today!
